About Us

At Shuttle Strikers, we are passionate about the exhilarating sport of badminton and are dedicated to creating a fun and inclusive environment for players of all ages and abilities. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or a seasoned player ready for some competitive action, we have something for everyone.

We believe that badminton is not just a sport but a way of life. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where players can come together, share their passion for the game, and forge lasting friendships. Our club is not just about improving your skills on the court, but also about fostering a sense of belonging and community off the court.

What Can We Offer?

  • Convenient location in central Manchester
  • Access to equipment such as shuttlecocks and racquets
  • Skilled and friendly coaching staff
  • Professionally maintained badminton courts
  • Regular club sessions throughout the week
  • Affordable membership options
  • Social events and tournaments
